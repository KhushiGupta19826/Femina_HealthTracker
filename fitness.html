Feel Good Workouts for Every Phase of Your Cycle

Optimize your fitness journey by working with your body's natural rhythm. Discover the perfect workouts tailored to each phase of your menstrual cycle.

Menstrual Phase

Days 1-5

Energy Level: Lower

Gentle Yoga & Light Walking

During your period, focus on gentle movement that soothes cramps and boosts your mood. Listen to your body and rest when needed.

Pro Tips

  • Child's pose and cat-cow stretches can help relieve cramps
  • Aim for 15-30 minutes of gentle movement daily
  • Prioritize self-care and adequate rest

Follicular Phase

Days 6-13

Energy Level: Rising

Cardio & Strength Training

As estrogen rises, your energy and strength increase. This is an excellent time to challenge yourself with more intense workouts.

Pro Tips

  • Great time for HIIT, running or dance cardio
  • Focus on building strength with moderate weights
  • Try new workout classes or routines

Ovulation Phase

Days 14-21

Energy Level: Peak

High-Intensity & Group Activities

During ovulation, you're at your physical peak. Take advantage of your maximum energy and endurance levels.

Pro Tips

  • Ideal time for personal records and challenges
  • Try team sports or group fitness classes
  • Focus on explosive movements and power training

Luteal Phase

Days 22-28

Energy Level: Decreasing

Strength Training & Pilates

As progesterone rises, your body temperature increases and energy starts to decline. Moderate your intensity and focus on controlled movements.

Pro Tips

  • Strength training can help manage PMS symptoms
  • Focus on form and controlled movements
  • Pilates and barre classes are excellent choices
