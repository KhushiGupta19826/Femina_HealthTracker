Femina - Frequently Asked Questions

What is Femina?
Femina is a menstrual health and wellness tracking app that helps you monitor periods, symptoms, moods, and access helpful resources.
Can I track my symptoms and mood?
Yes! Femina lets you log daily symptoms, mood changes, and flow levels to better understand your cycle.
Is Femina free to use?
Absolutely. Femina is completely free to use and doesn’t require any subscription.
Does Femina predict my next period?
Yes, it uses your past data to estimate your next period, fertile window, and PMS phase.
Will Femina work offline?
Currently, Femina is a static web app and some features may not work offline. Future updates will improve this!
